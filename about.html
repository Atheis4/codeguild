Andrew Theis

Gallery WIP About

Born in Toledo, Ohio in 1988--Andrew showed an affinity for the arts from a young age. He moved to Oregon in 2006 to attend Willamette University and completed his BA in Studio Art in 2011.

Art Work

Andrew's artistic voice didn't begin to form until his senior year in college. His thesis was inspired by the newsmedia and grafitti techniques--specifically stenciling.

His love of physics and mathematics wouldn't creep into his work until the summer of 2013 when he began the Cairo Pentagon series: an ongoing exploration of pattern, quantum physics, and relativism.

Andrew is currently enrolled in PDX Code Guild's full-time bootcamp. He hopes to explore the confluence of computer programming and his art.
